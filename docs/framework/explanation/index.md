# Explanation

Conceptual content that explains why Gofra works the way it does.

Explanation pages discuss tradeoffs, alternatives considered, and the mental
models behind the framework's design. They help you understand the reasoning,
not accomplish a task.

---

*Explanation pages will be added as the framework matures. For deep
architectural rationale, see the numbered design documents in `docs/` (these
are maintainer/design docs, not end-user reference).*
